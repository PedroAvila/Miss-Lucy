
/*  Adjetivos Demostrativos */

Near => Cerca  Singular => This(este, esta, esto) is * Plural => These(estas, estos) are

Far => Lejos   Singular => That(eso, esa, ese)    is * Plural => Those(esos, esas)   are 

1. this is a blue car 
- that is a blue car 

2. This is an apple 
- That is an apple 

3. This is my friend Jhon
- That is my friend Jhon


1. These are two new cars
- Those are two new cars

2. These are red apples
- Those are red apples 

3. These are my friends
- Those are my friends
