
/* El presente simple / Simple present tense */

1ra person => I, {We}



2da person => You, {You} 


3ra person => He, she, it { they }

Regla para la tercera persona singular
    (Solo oraciones afirmativas)
Se agrega "S"

She calls Juan 

He drinks water 

We drink water 

I drink water 


Se agrega "ES" O-X-Z-S-SS-SH-CH

She goes to the park

He watches TV 

They watch tv 


Try => tries
Study => studies

Jhon studies english 

Have => has para las terceras personas

Carmen has a car 
They have a house
