
Connectors
==========

I mean => Osea/Me refiero a																									
She's not very friendly. I mean, she's not rude, but she's just quiet.																									
I mean, it's not that big a deal.																									
																									
On top of that => Encima de eso(En la mayoría de los casos se usa para quejarse)																									
He was late. On top of that, he forgot the tickets.																									
The hotel was expensive. On top of that, the room was dirty.																									
																									
Then again => Aunque pensándolo bien/Aunque por otro lado																									
I might go to the party. Then again, I'm pretty tired.																									
She seems nice. Then again, we just met her. 																									
																									
At the end of the day => Cuando todo esta dicho y hecho/Al final de cuentas/Lo que realmente importa es																									
At the end of the day, you have to do what makes you happy.																									
We argued a lot. but at the end of the day, we're still family																									
																									
By the way(BTW) => Por cierto/A propósito/Ya que estamos																									
By the way, did you finish that report?																									
By the way, did you finish that report?																									
																									
Besides => Además de eso/Aparte de eso																									
I don't feel like going out. Besides, it's raining.																									
I'm not hungry. Besides, we just ate.																									
																									
To be fair => Para ser justos																									
She didn't help much. To be fair, she was feeling sick.																									
He's always late. To be fair, he has three kids and a crazy job.																									
																									
In fact => De hecho/En realidad/Es mas																									
He's a great cook. In fact, he used to work in a restaurant.																									
I'm not tired. In fact, I feel amazing.																									
																									
Anyway => De todas formas/De todos modos																									
Anyway, as I was saying, we need to finish the project by next Friday.																									
Anyway, I should get going.																									
I didn't really like the movie, anyway.																									
																									
That said => Aun así/Igual/De todos modos																									
He's very talented. That said, he still needs to practice more.																									
I love living in the city. That said, sometimes I miss the quiet of the countryside.
